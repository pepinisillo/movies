Overview

After a mysterious virus wipes out most of humanity, only children under the age of thirteen are spared and taken underground by vampires as livestock. Yuichiro Hyakuya, one of the surviving children, swears to exterminate all vampires after a tragic escape attempt, joining the Moon Demon Company to fight back in a world ruled by monsters and demons.
