Biography

Yuuichirou is a member of the Japanese Imperial Demon Army and Shinoa Hiragi's squad in the Moon Demon Company. Along with Mikaela Hyakuya, he possesses the "Seraph" gene, which is tied to the forbidden taboo and the central point of the story "Seraph of the End".

Yuuichirou seems to be an excellent swordsman, since he was trained by Guren himself. He is also a "seraph of the end", with a tenth of his being that is not human; it may be that he was subjected to experiments by the Hyakuya, but this isn't clear, as the adults responsible for him were murdered minutes after he arrived at the orphanage.
